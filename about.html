Who is Jaxon?

I earned my bachelor's degree in Management Information Systems from Brigham Young University, where I developed a strong foundation in both business and technology. During my studies, I discovered a passion for problem solving, system design, and building technology that creates real, practical impact for the people using it.

I'm currently pursuing a Master's degree at Utah State University with a focus on Web Development and Cybersecurity, with an expected graduation in April 2027. This program allows me to deepen my technical skills while also strengthening my understanding of secure, scalable, and user-centered digital experiences.

Outside of work and school, I enjoy running, reading, baking, movies, music, and spending quality time with my wife and our baby boy!

Looking ahead, my goal is to grow into a leadership role within a technology organization that values integrity, collaboration, and meaningful impact. I'm motivated by opportunities to mentor others, contribute to thoughtful decision-making, and help teams build solutions that genuinely make a difference.
